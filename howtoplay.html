How to play

Clicking

Clicking the orange clickable will grant 1 point. These points can be spent to buy generators, and the goal of the game is to get as many points as possible.

Generators

There are 5 generators. They cost points, and make more points, but the price for that generator will increase by 1.2x. To switch the generator you are buying, use the left/right arrow keys.

Defrag

When you defrag a generator, its amount and price are reset, but you gain a multiplier to that generator with the formula (x/5)^(1/1.7) where x is the amount you bought of that generator.
